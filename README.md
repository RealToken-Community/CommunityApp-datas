A collaborative open-source registry to document off-chain contributions (docs, tools, bots, etc.) with a year, a category, an author, a score, and a public proof.
example:
| Year | Category | Subject  | Author  | Score | Proof                                             |
|------|----------|----------|---------|-------|---------------------------------------------------|
| 2026 | Writer   | GitBook  | @PhilP  | 1000  | https://community-realt.gitbook.io/tuto-community |
